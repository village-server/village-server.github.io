How it Works

✻

Instead of relying on centralized cloud services, our service uses the idle resources of personal computers worldwide to perform the same tasks. We distribute server code for execution to everyday computer users who have installed our app, using excess computer resources to do the job.

Your Application

Your app calls cloud functions over HTTP to interact with your back-end.

Village Platform

We figure out what computers are on the network and route your traffic to them.

Home Computers

Our network of home computers execute your code and return a response.

Our Features

✻

We securely run your code on our serverless platform. You are only charged for the compute resources used at runtime. Since we know computers on our network are coming online and offline, we manage where your code will run so you don't lose a response and every request is received.

Security

Our technology encrypts all traffic to and from our network and we implement fraud detection to ensure our responses are genuine.

Reliability

We use the latest container orchestration systems with intelligent load balancing and automatic failure detection and recovery to guarantee your apps are always online. Backup cloud resources are used when our network of home PCs can't fulfull demand.

Scalability

Don't worry about provisioning new servers -- our system scales to handle your traffic automatically so your app will remain online no matter how much traffic you get.
